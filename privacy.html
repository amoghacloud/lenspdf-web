Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Period.

1. Data Collection

LensPDF is designed as an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal documents, scanned images, or PDF files to any external server.

2. Local Processing

All image processing, including cropping, filtering, and OCR (Text Recognition), is performed locally on your device using native APIs and on-device machine learning models.

3. Camera & Storage Permissions

We request access to your device's Camera and Storage solely for the purpose of capturing documents and saving the generated PDFs to your file system. These permissions are not used for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use anonymous analytics (e.g., Google Analytics or Firebase) to track crash reports and app performance. No personally identifiable information (PII) is linked to this data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
