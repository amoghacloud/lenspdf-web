Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchase". Ensure you are logged into the same Samsung account used to buy the app.

Is my data safe?

Yes. LensPDF processes all documents offline on your device. We do not upload your files to any cloud server.
